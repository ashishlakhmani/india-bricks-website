Our Process

From Clay to Construction

Every India Marka brick goes through a careful 4-step process to ensure the quality and strength our customers depend on.

01
🏔️

Clay Selection

We source only the best local clay from Gorakhpur — rich in minerals, free of impurities — for strong, durable bricks.

→
02
🤲

Table Moulding

Skilled workers hand-shape each brick in wooden moulds — giving them the uniform size and smooth finish builders prefer.

→
03
☀️

Sun Drying

Moulded bricks are air-dried naturally in the sun for several days, reducing moisture before firing.

→
04
🔥

Kiln Firing

Bricks are fired at high temperatures in our kiln, giving them the hardness, compressive strength, and the signature India mark.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything builders and homeowners ask us before placing an order.

Our minimum order is 1,000 bricks. We welcome orders of all sizes — from a single truckload for a homeowner to lakhs of bricks for large commercial projects.

We deliver across Gorakhpur and surrounding districts including Maharajganj, Deoria, Kushinagar, Siddharth Nagar, and other nearby areas. Contact us with your location to confirm delivery.

We typically require an advance payment at the time of order, with the balance due at delivery. For long-term contractor relationships, we can discuss flexible payment arrangements.

For in-stock orders within Gorakhpur, delivery is typically within 1–2 days. Larger orders or more distant locations may take 2–4 days. We will confirm the exact date when you place your order.

Our standard table moulded bricks are 230 mm × 110 mm × 70 mm (approximately 9" × 4.5" × 3"), which is the IS standard brick size used across India. We ensure consistent sizing in every batch.
