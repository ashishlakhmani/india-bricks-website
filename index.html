About Us

A Trusted Family Business in Gorakhpur

India Marka Eeta has been supplying quality table moulded bricks to builders and contractors across Gorakhpur and Eastern Uttar Pradesh for over two decades. Our bricks are made from carefully selected local clay, shaped by skilled workers, and kiln-fired to achieve the strength and uniformity that our customers depend on.

The "India" mark stamped on every brick is our promise of quality — a name builders recognise and trust on construction sites throughout the region.

We work directly with individual homebuilders, civil contractors, and large construction companies — offering fair, transparent pricing and reliable delivery so your project never faces a supply shortage.
